# MMFX Member App — What We've Built So Far

*Last updated: 17 June 2026*

## What it is
A private members' area for MarketMakersFX — a forex & gold (XAU/USD) trading academy. Members sign in with a magic link (no passwords), get a free trial, and unlock full access once they fund a partnered broker account ($500). It's a modern, branded web app (warm, premium look — orange accent).

## The look & feel
- Completely redesigned from the old dark "trading terminal" style into a warm, friendly, modern membership site.
- Consistent layout across every page: a left sidebar to navigate, clean cards, brand fonts.
- A redesigned **dashboard** (member home) with a live market ticker, a rotating spotlight of featured content, a "today's brief" (gold outlook + next class + last thing read), and a step-by-step "Analysis → Execution → Trade Management" journey.

## Member features (all built & access-controlled)
- **Indicators** — 10 trading indicators with TradingView links and preview covers.
- **eBook Library** — flagship "MM System" book plus a full shelf, with read-in-browser and download options.
- **Video Course** — 19 lessons organised into a 6-module syllabus, with progress tracking and downloadable slides.
- **Daily Analysis** — 16 video breakdowns, filterable by market bias and groupable by month or session, with PDF reports.
- **Signals** — a Telegram hub linking to the signals channel, performance, and community.
- **Strategies** — 2 published MM strategies with TradingView links and chart covers (more slots coming).
- **News & Articles** — live forex & macro news feed (gold, major pairs, oil), filterable.
- **Economic Calendar** — upcoming high/medium-impact events, timezone-aware.
- **Live Classes** — scheduling, managed by admin.
- **2 AI Bots** — "Know Your Style" and "Fundamental Desk," embedded in-app.
- **Admin panel** — verify deposits, grant trials, edit members, manage daily analysis & live classes.

## Status
- All of the above is **built, working, and saved** (committed to the project on the main branch).
- It currently runs locally for testing — not yet on a live public web address.
