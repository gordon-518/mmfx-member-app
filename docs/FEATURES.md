# Market Makers FX — Member App: Features & How to Use It

The MMFX member app is the home base for our traders. It pulls everything a
gold/forex trader needs — the market read, the tools to act on it, the education
to get better, and a way to track progress — into one warm, focused workspace.

This document explains **what's in the app, how a member uses each part, and how
it helps them trade, learn, and improve.**

---

## The big idea

Trading profitably isn't one skill — it's a loop:

> **Analysis → Execution → Trade Management**, built on a **foundation** of education.

The whole app is organised around that loop. The dashboard literally lays it out
as a pipeline, and every feature sits in the stage where a trader needs it.

---

## Getting in

- **Sign in with a magic link** — enter your email, click the link, you're in. No
  passwords to manage.
- **Trial → Member.** New members get a **14-day full-access trial**. When it ends,
  tools lock (they don't disappear). Funding a partnered broker account with $500
  — money that stays in *your* account, under *your* name — switches full access
  back on. (One re-trial is available if needed.)
- **Two access levels:**
  - **Full** — everything is open.
  - **Limited** (expired trial) — surfaces are visible but locked, with a clear
    path to restore access on the `/upgrade` page.

## The dashboard — your desk at a glance

The first thing you see, designed so you know *what matters right now* in seconds:

- **Live market bar** — running prices (gold, oil, BTC, FX) across the top.
- **Status hero** — your trial countdown or member status, with the right next step.
- **Spotlight** — a rotating feature card surfacing the timeliest thing: today's
  gold read, the next live class, the course, the Fundamental Desk.
- **Today's brief** — three quick reads: gold bias today, next live class, latest
  analysis.
- **The workflow pipeline** — *Everything you need to trade profitably*: the
  Analysis → Execution → Trade Management stages, each linking to its tools.
- **Foundations** — the course and library, the skill under all three stages.
- **Forex & macro news** — the latest market headlines with sentiment, linking
  into the full News page.

## What's in the app, stage by stage

### 1 · Analysis — *read the market*

| Feature | What it is | How it helps |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily Analysis** | A video read on gold, session by session — bias, key levels and the thesis behind them, with downloadable PDF reports. Filter by bias, group by month or session. | Start every day knowing the plan: what the desk expects and *why*. |
| **Fundamental Desk** | A live macro read on XAU/USD — the current fundamental picture (dollar, yields, risk sentiment, geopolitics) and a directional bias with conviction. | Understand the *forces* moving gold, not just the chart. |
| **News & Articles** | Real-time forex + commodities headlines, each tagged with market sentiment. Filter by pair (gold, silver, EUR/USD, GBP/USD, and 15+ more) and sentiment. | Stay on top of what's driving the pairs you trade, all in one feed. |
| **Economic Calendar** | The week ahead — high/medium-impact releases (central-bank decisions, CPI, jobs) with forecast/previous/actual, a currency filter, and a timezone selector. | Never get caught off guard by a news event; plan around the catalysts. |

### 2 · Execution — *find & place the trade*

| Feature | What it is | How it helps |
|---|---|---|
| **Signals** | A Telegram hub: the main signals channel (entry, stop, target — written before the click), plus free signals, a weekly performance channel, and a community/feedback group. New to Telegram? One-tap App Store / Play links. | High-conviction calls to follow or learn from, with the track record in the open. |
| **Indicators** | The 10-strong MM TradingView indicator suite, granted to your TradingView account on request. | The same tools the desk uses, on your own charts. |
| **Strategies** | Backtestable MM TradingView strategy scripts (MM AMA SuperTrend, MM System 5m Entry, more coming) with their live chart/backtest covers. | Test the rules yourself — entries, exits and risk, encoded. |

### 3 · Trade Management — *manage the position*

| Feature | What it is | How it helps |
|---|---|---|
| **Live Classes** | Twice-weekly live sessions on the charts (Zoom), with upcoming and past listings. | Trade alongside the desk in real time; see management decisions as they happen. |
| **Know Your Style** | A short assessment that profiles your trader archetype — your edge and the leak costing you money. | Trade in a way that fits *you*, and fix the habit holding you back. |

### Foundations — *the skill under all three*

| Feature | What it is | How it helps |
|---|---|---|
| **The MM System Course** | The full curriculum as a 6-module syllabus — Foundations → Reading Price → The Institutional Edge → Building Your Bias → The Entry Model → Managing & Routine — 19 video lessons with slide decks. The player stays on the page; your progress is saved so you can pick up where you left off. | Learn the system end to end, in order, at your pace. |
| **eBook Library** | The written system and references — the MM System eBook, the Five-Stage Workflow, Decision Trees & Invalidation, and the Cheat Sheets — to read in-browser or download. | The desk reference you keep open while you trade. |

## How it helps a trader — trade, learn, improve

**Trade.** Everything needed to act is one click away and current: the daily read
and live macro bias tell you *what* and *why*; the news feed and economic calendar
keep you ahead of catalysts; signals, indicators and strategies give you the *how*;
live classes cover managing the position.

**Learn.** The course teaches the complete MM System in a structured path (not a
pile of videos), the library is the written reference, and live classes turn theory
into watching it done on real charts.

**Improve.** The course tracks what you've watched and lets you resume; Know Your
Style surfaces the specific leak to fix; the weekly performance channel keeps the
signals' track record honest; and the Daily SOP lesson gives a repeatable routine
— mark, bias, classify, wait, confirm, execute, manage, journal.

## Notes on the experience

- **One consistent workspace** — a persistent sidebar, warm-premium design, and the
  same calm layout on every page. Works on desktop and mobile.
- **Built around the trader's day** — the dashboard answers "what now?", and each
  feature lives where you'd reach for it in the workflow.
- **Run by the desk** — daily analysis, live classes and content are kept current
