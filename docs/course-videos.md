## Module descriptions (card copy — use verbatim)

- **0 Golden Mindset (intro):** The trader's mindset before the mechanics — discipline, patience, and the psychology behind consistent execution. *(intro; confirm inclusion)*
- **1 XAUUSD Explained:** Gold as a tradable instrument — why XAUUSD moves differently from forex pairs.
- **2 What Moves Gold?:** The macro drivers of gold prices — the US Dollar, real yields, risk sentiment, and geopolitical tension.
- **3 Market Structure Part 1:** Reading higher-highs and higher-lows — how to identify trend direction from price action alone.
- **4 Market Structure Part 2:** Internal vs external structure, and the critical difference between a Break of Structure (BOS) and a Change of Character (CHoCH).
- **5 MM Core Concepts:** The pillars of the MM System at a glance — liquidity, order blocks, fair value gaps, and displacement.
- **6 Liquidity Deep Dive:** Where institutional orders hide — buyside/sellside liquidity, equal highs/lows, and how to spot a sweep in real time.
- **7 Displacement Mastery:** What separates real institutional intent from noise — body size, consecutive candles, FVGs, and structural breaks.
- **8 Daily Bias Part 1:** Calling direction for the day — mapping external liquidity on Daily and 4H, and defining your target.
- **9 Daily Bias Part 2:** Timeframe alignment, invalidation levels, and what to do when higher timeframes disagree.
- **10 15m Decision Framework:** The 15-minute playbook — trend day vs reversal day, and how to mark your entry zone (OB/FVG).
- **11 5m Confirmation Framework:** Waiting for a 5m CHoCH with displacement inside your 15m zone — the permission slip to drop to 1m.
- **12 Sessions and Time of Day:** Asian accumulation, London manipulation, New York distribution — and the kill zones.
- **13 1m Execution Framework:** The final entry trigger — 1m CHoCH or confirmation inside the Optimal Trade Entry (OTE) zone.
- **14 Breaker Blocks Mastery:** Failed order blocks that flip role — one of the highest-probability zones in the entire MM System.
- **15 News, Macro, Geopolitical Drivers:** Handling CPI, FOMC, NFP and geopolitical shocks — pre-event prep, kill rules, and when to stand aside.
- **16 Trade Management:** Break-even triggers, partial profits, trailing, and scaling — how to let winners run without giving it back.
- **17 Complete Workflow:** Every step from blank chart to closed trade — the full MM System in one end-to-end session.
- **18 Your Daily SOP:** The 8-step ritual for every trading day — mark, bias, classify, wait, confirm, execute, manage, journal.
